# Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/). By participating, you agree to abide by its terms.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces — issues, pull requests, discussions, and any other interactions in this repo or in official Blind Insight communication channels related to this project.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness behavior that violates the Contributor Covenant, please report it to **conduct@blindinsight.com**. Reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and confidentially.

The maintainers are obligated to respect the privacy and security of the reporter. We may take any action we deem appropriate, up to and including a permanent ban from the project.

## Maintainers

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing the standards of acceptable behavior. They may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that don't align with this Code of Conduct.
